Why I decided to take a cruise through the Bahamas

I spent one year living in the USA from Dec 2009 to Dec 2010. I went there as an au pair and I had (still have) great friends!

In July of 2010 me and my friends decided to do something different for the 4th of July (USA Independence Day) and we thought it would be fun to go on a cruise through the Bahamas!

We were super excited and we planned everything, so we would not have any surprises along the way.

But, of course, life is a box full of surprises and we got stuck in the highway and we missed our flight!!

Fortunately, we were able to book a next-day flight (otherwise we would not have enough time to get in the cruise on time, since we were travelling from Michigan to Miami!). We paid almost another ticket, we had to spend the night at the airport (sleeping on the floor, on the chairs, etc) but we got to travel on the first class (yayy, a first for me) and it was totally worth it.

Of course we have learned a big lesson, and I never missed a flight again after this experience hahahaha.
