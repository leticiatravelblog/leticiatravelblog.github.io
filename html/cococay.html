About Cococay

After one day travelling on the cruise down to the Bahamas, the first island we visited was Cococay.

Cococay is one of the Berry Islands, a collection of cays and small islands and is located approximately 55 miles north of Nassau.

We spent the day there, taking pictures and relaxing on the beach.

As you can see in some pictures the sky looks dark because (as we have been told by the people of the island) in July the hurricane season starts! Not really good for us since it was our first time on a cruise, but everything turned out just fine! The cruise did not sink. Yay!

Apart from the beach, in Cococay they also have floating 'toys' to play with, in the ocean. Of course we had to pay to use it, so unfortunately we just took some pictures. As far as I remember, the price was around $40.00 for half an hour.

I hope you enjoy the pictures below! The place is awesome and I am sorry that the pictures do not show that (we took it with a bad camera... come on, it was in 2010, iphone had just started to become famous whoohoo).
