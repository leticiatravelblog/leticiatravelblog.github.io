About Nassau

After visiting Cococay we went to the capital of Bahamas: Nassau.

The city has an estimated population of 274,400 as of 2016, or 70 percent of the entire population of the Bahamas.

We spent the day walking around the city, visiting the little shops and taking pictures of the colourful houses.

After that we came back to the cruise to enjoy the last day of our trip!
